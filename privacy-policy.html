Privacy Policy

Last updated: September 27, 2025

Your privacy is important. This add-on, Text Styles, does not collect, store, or share any personal information.

Data Access

The add-on only accesses your Google Docs content as necessary to apply styles and formatting that you request. All processing happens entirely within your Google account using Google Apps Script.

Data Collection

This add-on does not collect, transmit, or store your documents, content, or personal information on any external servers.

Third Parties

The add-on does not share information with third parties.

Changes

If this policy changes, the updated version will be posted here with a revised “Last updated” date.
